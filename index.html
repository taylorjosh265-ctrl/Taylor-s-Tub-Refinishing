Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions customers ask before booking bathtub reglazing.

Is bathtub refinishing worth it?

Yes. If the tub is structurally sound, refinishing can give it a clean, refreshed look without replacing the entire bathtub.

Do I need to replace my bathtub?

Not always. Many stained, dull, chipped, or peeling tubs can be repaired and refinished instead of replaced.

Can you fix peeling?

Yes. Peeling areas are addressed during prep so the tub can be properly repaired and refinished.

How do I get the fastest estimate?

Call or message and send clear photos of the tub, including any peeling, chips, stains, drain damage, or worn areas.
